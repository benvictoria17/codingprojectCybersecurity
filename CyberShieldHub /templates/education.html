Learn About Cybersecurity

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity means keeping computers, phones, and information safe from bad people who want to steal or break them.

Just like you lock your house door to keep burglars out, cybersecurity helps lock your digital life to keep hackers out!

Why Learn About Cybersecurity?

  • Keep your personal information private
  • Protect your accounts from hackers
  • Use the internet safely
  • Help your family stay safe online
  • Learn about cool technology

Basic Cybersecurity Steps For Kids

Use Strong Passwords

Make passwords that are hard to guess. Use letters, numbers, and symbols.

Keep Personal Info Private

Don't share your full name, address, phone number, or school name online.

Use Security Software

Make sure your computer has anti-virus software that is up to date.

Be Careful with Emails

Don't open emails or attachments from people you don't know.

Download with Care

Only download apps and files from trusted sources.

Ask for Help

If something seems strange online, ask a trusted adult for help.
