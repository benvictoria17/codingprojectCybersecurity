OSINT Information Gathering

What is OSINT?

OSINT means "Open Source Intelligence." It is information that anyone can find on the internet without special tools.

This tool helps you see what information about you or someone else is public on the internet.

OSINT Scanner

Example: johndoe@example.com or example.com

What Can OSINT Find?

For People

  • Social media accounts
  • Photos shared online
  • Work information
  • Location information
  • Phone numbers

For Websites

  • Who owns the website
  • Server information
  • Connected websites
  • Email addresses
  • Technology used

Stay Safe Online

Remember these safety tips:

Protect Personal Info

Be careful about what personal information you share online. Once it's on the internet, it can be hard to remove.

Use Privacy Settings

Check the privacy settings on your social media accounts. Make your accounts private if possible.

Location Sharing

Turn off location sharing on apps and photos when you don't need it.

Google Yourself

Search for your own name online to see what information is public. This can help you find things to remove.
