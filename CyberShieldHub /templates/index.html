Why Is Cybersecurity Important?

Cybersecurity is like having a strong lock on your house. It keeps bad people out and your stuff safe.

With good cybersecurity, you can:

  • Keep your personal information private
  • Stop people from stealing your accounts
  • Protect your computer from viruses
  • Stay safe while using the internet
